About Us

Welcome to Shannon's Cakes!

Shannon's Cakes is a family owned bakery that is best known for not being featured on the TLC hit show Cake Boss.

What once was a small cake stand turned into a world famous cake store overnight after Kim Kardashian posted it on her Instagram story

Because of the Shannon's family’s dedication to quality and excellence, Shannon’s has received national recognition. Master Baker Shannon has been featured in numerous publications, such as Modern Bride, and The Knot. Shannon’s cake design was voted by the Today’s Show viewers as best cake in America. He has also appeared in many other media outlets, including multiple appearances on the Food Network, for his intricate sugar art designs specialty cakes and wedding cakes that look just as good as they taste.
